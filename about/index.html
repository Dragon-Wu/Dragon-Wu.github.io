Hello, world. Hello, friends

Hello,

I am a PhD candidate at Zhejiang University, and my advisors are Prof.Changzheng Yuan and Prof. Jie Yang. My major is Public health and Preventive medicine, and my research interests mainly focus on healthcare data analysis. Actually, I am a newbie in this area, but fortunately, my advisors and teammates are very nice!

Previously, I received an M.S. degree in translational medicine at the School of Public Health, Xiamen University. During my master’s period, I mainly focused on cryo-EM image processing.

In the future, I hope to do some interesting, meaningful, and useful work in medicine and make a small contribution to human health!

I like badminton and billiards, and I also like Dota2, although I have not played it for a long time…

If you see here, you are probably interesting and free, and such people usually have more luck :) I also wish you happy and healthy every day!

Everything is going well.

Best regards!
